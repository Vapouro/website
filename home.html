Hi, I'm Sean!

I'm a Sydney-based animation student currently studying a Bachelor of Fine Arts, majoring in Animation at UNSW Art & Design. I'm super passionate about storytelling and showcasing that through the medium of animation, whether it be 2D or 3D. I especially love working as a background artist, character animator, and storyboarder!

Favourite Projects

Major Work - Game Night

Game Night was my HSC Visual Arts Major Work, created in my final year of high school. It tells the story of three kids who get trapped inside a Dungeons & Dragons-inspired board game and must battle their way out. The work achieved a Band 6 and was selected for the NEXT 2024 exhibition.

University Assignment - Bloom

Bloom is my first university short film, created using 2D animation and experimental backgrounds. It follows a lone dandelion left behind after a group of flowers bloom and float away, until a group of forest critters band together to help it break free, told entirely through sound effects and without dialogue.
